## Overview
The PMS (Preventive Maintenance Service) Inquiry System allows sales agents to manage customer PMS requests and communicate with customers about their vehicle maintenance needs.

## How to Access the PMS System

### Step 1: Navigate to PMS Inquiries
1. Log in to your sales agent account
2. Go to the main dashboard
3. Look for the **"PMS Inquiries Management"** section or navigate to `/pages/agent_pms_inquiries.php`

### Step 2: View All PMS Inquiries
The PMS Inquiries page displays:
- **Customer Name** - Who submitted the PMS request
- **Vehicle Model** - The vehicle being serviced
- **Plate Number** - Vehicle identification
- **PMS Type** - Type of maintenance service
- **Status** - Current status (Open, In Progress, Resolved)
- **Unread Messages** - Badge showing new customer messages
- **Total Messages** - Total conversation count

## How to Communicate with Customers

### Step 1: Open the Chat
1. On the PMS Inquiries page, find the inquiry you want to respond to
2. Click the **"View Messages"** button (red button with chat icon)
3. This opens the PMS Inquiry Chat page

### Step 2: View Inquiry Details
On the left sidebar, you'll see:
- **Customer Information** - Name, email, phone number
- **Vehicle Details** - Model, plate number, PMS type
- **Customer Needs** - The customer's description of their vehicle issues
- **Current Status** - Open, In Progress, or Resolved

### Step 3: Send Messages to Customer
1. Scroll to the bottom of the chat area
2. Type your message in the **"Type your message here..."** text box
3. Click the **"Send"** button (or press Enter)
4. Your message will appear in the chat with a timestamp

### Step 4: Receive Customer Messages
- Customer messages appear on the left side of the chat (gray bubbles)
- Agent messages appear on the right side (red bubbles)
- Messages are automatically marked as read when you view them
- Unread message count updates in real-time

## Assigning Inquiries

### Auto-Assignment
- When you click "View Messages" on an unassigned inquiry, it automatically assigns to you
- The status changes to "In Progress"

### Manual Assignment
1. On the PMS Inquiries page, find an unassigned inquiry
2. Click the **"Assign to Me"** button
3. The inquiry is now assigned to you

## Managing Inquiry Status

You can update the status of an inquiry:
- **Open** - New inquiry, not yet addressed
- **In Progress** - Currently being handled
- **Resolved** - Issue resolved, customer satisfied

## Best Practices

1. **Respond Promptly** - Check for new inquiries regularly
2. **Be Clear** - Provide detailed information about PMS services
3. **Ask Questions** - If customer needs are unclear, ask for clarification
4. **Provide Solutions** - Offer specific maintenance recommendations
5. **Follow Up** - Keep customers updated on their PMS status

## Troubleshooting

### I can't see the "View Messages" button
- Make sure you're logged in as a Sales Agent
- Refresh the page
- Check that the inquiry has been created (should have a customer name)

### Messages aren't sending
- Make sure the message is not empty
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### I don't see any inquiries
- Check if there are any PMS requests submitted by customers
- Make sure you're on the correct page: `/pages/agent_pms_inquiries.php`

## Contact Support
